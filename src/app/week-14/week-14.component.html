Summary

This week, I created the client and tasker homepages with intuitive swipe-card interactions and finalized their designs by adding Settings and Address widgets. I implemented advanced filtering—distance via real-time geolocation, specialization (e.g., plumber, developer), and client-specific age filters—using a new Address table and geoconvert to translate coordinates into readable locations. Integrating geolocation proved challenging in ensuring consistent, accurate tracking, and balancing multiple filters without cluttering the interface required careful UX design. Fetching and storing user preferences for filtering also took longer than expected but ultimately strengthened my data modeling and problem-solving skills. Overall, these enhancements significantly improve the platform’s interactivity and location-aware capabilities.
